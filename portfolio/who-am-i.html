About

My name is Jin Jin Heipler. I'm just a small, non-binary person, adopted from China and raised in Shrewsbury, Massachusetts. I'm currently in my second year and studying New Media Interactive Development at RIT. I chose this major because I wanted something that allows me to combine my creativity and my programming skills to create exciting experiences. My dream job would involve developing some of the interactive exhibits in museums or at Universal Studios.

When I'm not doing schoolwork, you can usually find me in the gym, the track, or the rugby pitch. I'm a third degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do, sprinter, and rugger. I truly believe that a sound body is a sound mind. I also try to stay active in the LGBTQ+ community and help advocate for change and equal rights.
